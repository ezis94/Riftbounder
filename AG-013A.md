## Targeting update

A target must be targetable both when selected and when the chain item resolves.
If it becomes untargetable before resolution, its resolution status is
`Untargetable`; instructions concerning it are skipped and the rest of the
spell resolves as much as possible. If it becomes targetable again before
resolution, it is eligible again.

Deflect remains separate. It is evaluated only as a mandatory additional cost
when the target is chosen and is not re-evaluated during resolution.

## Current targetability boundary

The current board model exposes a single dynamic `IsTargetable` state. Future
work can refine this into source- and category-specific untargetability (for
example, enemy spells only) without changing the revalidation lifecycle.
