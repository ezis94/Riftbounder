# AG-008 — Resolve Finalized Spells

## Commit

`AG-008: resolve finalized spells into owner Trash`

## Implemented

- Spell-effect execution abstraction.
- No-op spell executor for effectless test spells.
- Resolver for finalized `PlayCardChainItem` objects.
- Resolution lifecycle:
  1. record resolution start;
  2. execute the spell effect;
  3. place the spell card in its owner's Trash;
  4. record successful completion.
- Failed engine execution:
  - records a failure event;
  - leaves the spell outside Trash;
  - returns a failed Chain resolution so the transactional Chain retains it.
- Chain integration tests.

## Rules mapped

- 108.2.b — Trash contains spells after execution.
- 359.3.c–d — Reaction opportunity, spell execution, then owner Trash.
- 359.3.e.10–11 — A spell can resolve with no effective instructions and is
  still considered played; instructions are followed as much as possible.

Canonical rules:
`https://www.riftbound.one/rules/riftbound-core-rules.pdf`

## Deliberate boundary

AG-008 provides only the spell lifecycle and effect-executor boundary. It does
not yet define individual spell instructions, targets, modes, mistargeting, or
effect attribution.

A failed `SpellExecutionResult` represents an engine-level inability to execute
the spell, not a normal rules outcome. A legal spell whose instructions do
nothing must return success and still move to Trash.
