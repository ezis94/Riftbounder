# AG-010 — Partial Targeted Effect Resolution

## Commit

`AG-010: resolve targeted spell instructions as much as possible`

## Implemented

- Data-defined spell instructions.
- Instruction target-index bindings.
- Instruction-handler registry.
- Per-instruction target revalidation.
- Eligible targets are affected.
- Missing or wrong-location targets are skipped.
- A spell with no remaining eligible targets still resolves successfully.
- A target that gains Deflect after finalization remains eligible.
- Missing handlers and malformed target indexes are engine failures.
- Execution reports expose:
  - each resolved instruction;
  - target eligibility results;
  - affected target counts.
- Full spell-lifecycle integration test proving partial resolution still sends
  the spell to its owner's Trash.

## Human-validated engine contract

- Targets are chosen and locked during play finalization.
- At resolution, instruction-specific conditions are checked again.
- Deflect is not checked again.
- If a unit targeted as "a unit at a battlefield" moves to Base, that target is
  skipped.
- The card performs as much as possible and completes normally.

## Deliberate boundary

The generic `AffectEligibleTargetsHandler` is a test and extension primitive.
AG-010 does not yet implement concrete game instructions such as Kill, Draw,
Move, Heal, Ready, Exhaust, or Buff.
