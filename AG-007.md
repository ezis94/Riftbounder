# AG-007 — Finalize and Play Spells

## Commit

`AG-007: finalize spell plays onto the Chain`

## Implemented

- Card definitions with type and printed resource cost.
- Backward-compatible legacy card construction for earlier tests.
- Play-card request and result models.
- Timing/permission abstraction.
- Chain-priority timing adapter.
- Spell-only play finalization:
  - confirm source card is in the player's hand;
  - confirm ownership;
  - confirm timing permission;
  - confirm declared resource payment;
  - remove the spell from hand;
  - pay its costs;
  - create a finalized Chain item;
  - establish or reset Chain priority.
- Transaction rollback for engine failures.
- Rune Pool payment refund primitive.
- Focused integration tests.

## Rules mapped

- 108.1 — Chain Zone
- 350–354 — Playing cards and moving the card to the Chain
- 356–357 — Total cost and payment
- 358 — Legality
- 359.1 and 359.3 — Finalized spell Chain item

Canonical rules:
`https://www.riftbound.one/rules/riftbound-core-rules.pdf`

## Deliberate boundary

This milestone supports Spells only.

Units and Gear leave the Chain during finalization and enter the Board under
rules 359.2. That requires Locations, Base permanents, battlefield ownership,
and ready/exhausted permanent state, which are deferred to a dedicated
permanent-play milestone.

Targets, modes, additional costs, cost modifiers, and spell execution are also
deferred.
